Life skills

I can peel a whole fruit with one long thin strip. I believe that as long as I am patient and calm, I can finish peeling a fruit without stopping.

My entire mind focuses on that strip when I try to peel fruits in one long, thin strip. As I empty my mind of other things, I am refreshed from the process of just peeling fruits.
I enjoy the feeling of success after I finish peeling the fruit in one strip. This feeling boosts my confidence. And I believe this skill benefits my mind.

Travel experience

When I was in elementary school, I traveled to an underdeveloped mountain area in Asia. Compared to the modern city where I lived, this poor area didn't make full use of its natural resources.

As the population grew, resources were becoming less available and pollution became an issue. I wanted to apply sustainable environment models to my community to make resources available to more people.

These stories were written in 2018 by myself based on my real experience.
